Biography

Ani DiFranco was born on September 23, 1970 in Buffalo, NY. Her music has been classified as both folk and alternative. However, many of her songs also include influences from punk, funk, hip hop, and jazz. She began performing at the age of nine and started writing songs by the time she was fourteen. At sixteen she graduated from Buffalo Academy of Visual and Performing Arts high school. After, she took courses at Buffalo State College. At the age of nineteen, she started her own record company, Righteous Babe Records. DiFranco has stated many times that she is anti-corporate and has demonstrated the importancce of this value system throughout her career.

After creating the label, Ani DiFranco also moved to NYC where she released her first, self-titled, album. Simultaneously, she began taking poetry classes at The New School. In total, DiFrano has released a total of 32 albums as of 2021. Her record label has also shown tremendous success. Growing organically, Righteous Babe Records now represents around twenty-three musicians.

For more information about Ani Difrano, and the other artists on Righteous Babe Records, visit: https://righteousbabe.com
